Questions

Answers, without the fluff.

Is this safe if I have sensitive skin? +

Yes — sensitive skin is exactly what Auréa was designed for. Every ingredient is screened for reactivity. We never use synthetic fragrance, parabens, sulfates, or alcohol-based astringents. We recommend a patch test on the inner arm first.

When will the steam pads be back in stock? +

Our next drop is within 2 weeks. Join the waitlist above and you'll get 24-hour early access before the public restock. Previous batches have sold out in under 4 days.

Can I use the gel during my period or postpartum? +

The V-Fresh Restore Gel is safe postpartum (after your 6-week clearance) and during menstruation. Many customers find it especially soothing during perimenopausal dryness. If pregnant, consult your OB first.

How is this different from a yoni wash or wipe? +

Washes and wipes clean the surface — often aggressively, disrupting pH and microbiome. Auréa works the opposite way: botanical warmth and plant humectants support the body's natural balance instead of overriding it. Restoration, not intervention.

How do I use the Steam Pad? +

Simply place the pad in your underwear. Your body heat activates the herbs — no water, no seat, no filters. Wear up to 3 hours. For regular issues, use every 2 weeks. For major concerns, once a week.
